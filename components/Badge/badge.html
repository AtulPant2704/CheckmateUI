Badge

Badge is present at top-right or bottom right of a container. It usually displays numbers or online / offline status depending on where they are used.

Badges on Avatars

Badges are used on Avatars to display the online, offline or away status of the user.

Badges on Icons

Badges are used on Icons to display the count of that specific property of an icon.
