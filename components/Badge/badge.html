Badge

Badge is present at top-right or bottom right of a container. It usually displays numbers or online / offline status depending on where they are used.

Badges on Avatars

Badges are used on Avatars to display the online, offline or away status of the user. 

                        
<div class="avatar-badge badge-off">
    <img src="../images/avatar-1.jpg" alt="avatar-image" class="round-img">
    <span class="round-badge badge-red"></span>
</div>
<div class="avatar-badge badge-on">
    <img src="../images/avatar-2.jpg" alt="avatar-image" class="round-img">
    <span class="round-badge badge-green"></span>
</div>
<div class="avatar-badge badge-away">
    <img src="../images/avatar-1.jpg" alt="avatar-image" class="round-img">
    <span class="round-badge badge-away"></span>
</div>

Badges on Icons

Badges are used on Icons to display the count of that specific property of an icon.
