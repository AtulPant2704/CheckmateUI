Typography

Documentation and text utilities which CheckMate follows that you can use in your project.

Heading

You can use heading from h1 to h6 for different headings.

Heading 1

Heading 2

Heading 3

Heading 4

Heading 5
Heading 6
                        
                            <h1>Heading 1</h1>
                            <h2>Heading 2</h2>
                            <h3>Heading 3</h3>
                            <h4>Heading 4</h4>
                            <h5>Heading 5</h5>
                            <h6>Heading 6</h6>

Paragraph

You can use different paragraph fonts for different font sizes like .body-cp-rg for regular font, .body-cp-lg for large font, .body-cp-md for medium font and .body-cp-sm for small font.

Pragraph body-copy-large

Paragraph body-copy-regular

Paragraph body-copy-medium

Paragraph body-copy-small

                        
                        <p class="p body-cp-lg">Pragraph body-copy-large</p>
                        <p class="p body-cp-rg">Paragraph body-copy-regular</p>
                        <p class="p body-cp-md">Paragraph body-copy-medium</p>
                        <p class="p body-cp-sm">Paragraph body-copy-small</p>

Alignment

You can use .align-left, .align-center and .align-right classes for aligning content to the left, center and right.

Align Left

Align Center

Align Right

                        
                            <h3 class="align-left">Align Left</h3>
                            <h3 class="align-center">Align Center</h3>
                            <h3 class="align-right">Align Right</h3>

Extra Styles

  • To make a text gray, use .gray-text class.
  • To add strike-through on text, use .text-strike class.
  • To underline a text, use .text-underline class.

This is a gray text.

This text has a line going through it.

This text is underlined.

                        
                        <p class="gray-text">This is a gray text.</p>
                        <p class="text-strike">This text has a line going through it.</p>
                        <p class="text-underline">This text is underlined.</p>
