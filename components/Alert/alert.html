Alert

An Alert is used to attract user's attraction to an important piece of information in a short and attractive way without breaking the user's flow.

Simple Alert

Primarly there are four types of alerts which can be used by adding alert-info, ' alert-success, alert-danger or alert-warning classes to the existing alert class.
