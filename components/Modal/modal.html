Modal

Modal is a popup that is displayed on a screen over entire web page to display important piece of information or to ask for a certain piece of action from a user.

Basic Modal

This is a simple modal displaying heading and sub-heading and ask among two choices from a user.

Modal Heading

Modal subheading displaying certain piece of information.

                        
<div class="modal-container">
    <h3 class="modal-heading">Modal Heading</h3>
    <p class="modal-subheading">Modal subheading displaying certain piece of information.</p>
    <div class="modal-btns">
        <button class="btn btn-solid-primary">Action 1</button>
        <button class="btn btn-outline-primary">Action 2</button>
    </div>
</div>
